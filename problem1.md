## Understanding Precast Yards

Precast Yards is a place where the concrete element ex: Beam, etc. are created and transported.

# Process in Precast Yards
* *Batching Plant* - The cement is mixed with material such as gravel, sand to form concrete.
* *Steel Fabrication* - The Steel bar is cut and folded to form a cage structure before being placed into the mold.
* *Casting Beds and Molds* - After placing the steel structure into mold the concrete mixture is poured.
* *Curing* - Give the mixture enough time to cure.
